BE - BACHELOR OF ENGINEERING

Bachelor of Engineering is the most sought after career option for students of the science stream. All the 25 branches of the study find relevance in the fields of industry, technology and business. Thousands of students every year aspire to be engineers. This is the course that demands well-read, experienced and up-to-date faculty, extraordinary infrastructure and adequate industrial exposure during the course. We at Shree Devi Institute of Technology are proud to present our institution as a premier engineering college. Our students are recruited by the leading IT companies like TCS, WIPRO, INFOSYS, Tech Mahindra, TCS, IBM, Birla Soft, CDC Software, Syntellect, Phoenix Maritime, Flexi Resources, I-Soft Technologies, SLK Software, SPAN Infotech, Xchanging and Global E-Soft Systems among others. We have achieved 100 % passes in Engineering results in VTU examinations with 90 % distinctions and 10% first classes in 2010-2011.


Courses Offered – BE

Shree Devi Institute of Technology offers a four year bachelor degree programme in Bachelor of Engineering (BE) in six branches


Electronics & Communication Engineering


Computer Science & Engineering


Information Science & Engineering


Mechanical Engineering


Aeronautical Engineering


Civil Engineering


Duration

The course is of four academic years duration, conducted in eight semesters with each semester of 16 weeks duration. The examination in all the subjects of all eight semesters is conducted at the end of each respective semester.

MBA - MASTER 0F BUSINESS ADMINISTRATION

The institution offers a two-year management programme leading to an MBA degree affiliated to Visveswaraya Technological University, Belgaum, Karnataka and recognised by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi.


The course is spread over four semesters with seven areas of specialisation in the management sector.


Marketing


Finance


Information System


Human Resource Management


Production


International Business


Banking & Finance


The examination in all the subjects of all four semesters shall be conducted at the end of each respective semester.


MCA - MASTER OF COMPUTER APPLICATION

Shree Devi Institute of Technology started, Masters in Computer Application, (affiliated to VTU & recognised by AICTE) to impart high quality formal education in computer science through a comprehensive 3 year masters programme. The course is exclusively designed to meet the IT requirements of IT trained students for various organisations. It grooms the students for a successful career in the IT Industry. The course emphasizes on planning, designing and building of complex commercial application software and system software. A three year’s full-time MCA includes a project (industrial training), interactive classes, national seminars and other activities. Students apply their skills to design application areas for transaction processing (such as banking, stock exchange order processing) simulation, database management, e-commerce, design support and data communications, networking, embedded technologies and the like.


Duration

The course shall be of three academic years’ duration and conducted in six semesters with each semester of 16 weeks’ duration. The examination in all the subjects of all six semesters is conducted at the end of each respective semester.
